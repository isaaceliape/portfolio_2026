Overview

splitfair is a web application that makes splitting bills and expenses among groups simple and fair. Whether you're managing a group trip, shared household expenses, or a dinner out with friends, splitfair handles the complex calculations and ensures everyone knows exactly what they owe.

Key Features

  • Smart Calculations: Automatically calculates fair shares and complex expense distributions
  • Multiple Groups: Manage multiple groups and their expenses separately
  • Settlement Summary: Clear breakdown of who owes whom and how much
  • Expense Tracking: Detailed history of all expenses and payments
  • Progressive Web App: Works offline and installable on mobile devices
  • Dark Mode: Easy on the eyes with full theme support

Technical Stack

Built with Vue.js for reactive UI updates and smooth interactions. The app uses PWA technology for offline functionality and seamless mobile experiences. Complex mathematical algorithms ensure accurate debt calculations even with uneven splits and multiple currencies.

Why I Built It

A practical tool born from real-world needs — keeping track of shared expenses can be stressful. splitfair eliminates the mental overhead of calculating who owes what, ensuring fairness and reducing potential conflicts among friends and family.
